# Recommended Advanced Components for Home Page

## 1. **Last Read / Bookmarks Widget**
- Shows last 3 Surahs read with timestamps
- Quick resume buttons
- Progress indicators (ayah position)

## 2. **Daily Ayah of the Day**
- Random inspirational Quranic verse
- Translation and transliteration
- Share button
- Refresh daily

## 3. **Upcoming Duaa/Azkar Alert**
- Next dua category recommendations
- Based on time of day (morning, noon, evening, night)
- Quick 1-tap access

## 4. **Islamic Calendar Widget**
- Current Hijri date and Islamic month
- Days remaining in current month
- Islamic holidays/important dates

## 5. **Quick Stats Dashboard**
- Total surahs read (session)
- Total ayahs read
- Favorite qari/reciter
- Weekly reading streak

## 6. **Prayer Time Countdown Card** (Already exists - enhance)
- Add next prayer name
- Add prayer duration
- Add make-up prayer tracker

## 7. **Zakat Calculator Link**
- Quick access to Zakat calculator
- Current year zakat status

## 8. **Notification Settings Card**
- Prayer time reminders toggle
- Daily ayah notification
- Customize reminder times

## 9. **Qibla Direction Mini Widget**
- Quick compass icon
- Shows current Qibla bearing
- Link to full Qibla page

## 10. **Recently Viewed Duas**
- History of last 5 duas viewed
- Quick re-read buttons

## Implementation Priority:
1. ⭐⭐⭐ Last Read Widget (High impact, easy)
2. ⭐⭐⭐ Daily Ayah (High engagement)
3. ⭐⭐ Islamic Calendar (Good info)
4. ⭐⭐ Quick Stats (Gamification)
5. ⭐ Prayer duration (Enhancement)
6. ⭐ Others (Nice to have)
